My computer gaming journey

How did this all start?

I grew up with very minimal electronics and mainly got my intertainment from outdoor activities untill i got my first xbox 360 at the age of 16. I will admit I was extremely addicted. There would be nights that I would be up until 3am playing. im sure im not the first boy to like games that much at that age considering that all my friends were just as addicted.

The pursuasion I wont regret.

I had already known my friend steven had built a pc and that was mainly because he would hardly ever play on xbox with me. I did'nt worry about it too much because i did have alot of other friends on xbox still. It seemed at the time that switching to building a pc and gaming on that was becoming a big thing. I remember there were occasional times when steven would play on xbox again for short times to try and convince me to build a gaming pc. He would always mention how much faster, easier, and over all better it was but I typically blew him off because pc gaming was very expensve.

The final switch

Being around the age that I was I was very stubborn and would often not change my mind even if i knew I was wrong. I still did not want to switch to pc, that is untill I used Stevens computer. From there on out I knew within ten minutes of playing that I wanted a gaming pc. Of course, another downfall of being you is that you often dont have much money for any at all. I did have a job but it was part time so I knew i was not good at saving money so I had to come up with a plan. The only way for me to commit was buy it piece by piece and untill i had all the required components. It took me about a year to finish it to where i could play and I was more than happy that i had switched, but I did'nt stop there. I got sort of obsessed with not only pc gaming but the look and feel of it. So, here I am 6000 dollars later, and about 5 years later. I have my monster rig of a gaming pc and I love it.
